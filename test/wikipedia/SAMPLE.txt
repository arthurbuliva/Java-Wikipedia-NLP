Paper is a thin material produced by pressing together moist fibres of cellulose pulp derived from wood, rags or grasses, and drying them into flexible sheets.
It is a versatile material with many uses, including writing, printing, packaging, cleaning, and a number of industrial and construction processes.

Karatasi (kutoka Kigiriki Χαρτί kharti kupitia lugha ya Kiarabu قرطاص qartas) ni laha bapa na nyembamba ya konge za mimea zilizokandamizwa na kushikamana.
Watu huandika kwenye karatasi, vitabu na magazeti hufanywa kwa karatasi, tena vipande vikubwa vya karatasi hutumika kwa kufunga vitu ndani yake.
